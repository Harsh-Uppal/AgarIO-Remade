How to start the game
Enter your name in the Enter Name text box and click on play button
Goal of the game
When you start the game you are small, you have to eat to grow bigger. Eat other blobs whose size is small. The goal is to become the biggest.
Controls
Movement Controls:
W to move up
A to move left
S to move down
D to move right

Esc to exit
World Border
You have to stay in the world border or you will die. The world border is drawn with red color. When you are out of the world border a warning icon is shown on the top right corner of your screen.
